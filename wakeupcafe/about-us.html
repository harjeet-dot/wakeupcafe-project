Welcome to The WakeUp Cafe

It all started 10 years ago, when Bajwa family of 4 sat around their breakfast table in their modest kitchen, with cups of coffee in their hands and mum’s delicious blueberry muffins and elderflower cake on the table. The reason for this family meeting was to come up with a plan to leave behind their demanding yet unfulfilling 9 to 5 jobs and follow their passion and dreams. All 4 of them shared great passion for good coffee and delicious food from around the globe. They all were well known for their amazing cooking skills and the joy they got from feeding friends and family. Thus, came the idea of “Wake Cup” café. Our Café is a family run business. And we pride ourselves in serving our customers the best coffee, quality food, exceptional service and a unique coffee house environment. We have been a part of the local tight-knit community. We are thankful to our staff and customers for their loyalty. We look forward to serve you at our Café.
